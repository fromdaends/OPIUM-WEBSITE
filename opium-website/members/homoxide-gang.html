Homixide Gang

About Artist

Homixide Gang is a dynamic duo under the Opium Label, known for their high-energy performances and unique sound. Their music blends trap influences with innovative beats, captivating audiences worldwide.

Biography

Homixide Gang, hailing from Atlanta, Georgia, has quickly risen to prominence in the music industry. With their distinct style and dedication to their craft, they have become a staple in the modern trap scene.

Music

Snotty World (2021)

The debut album that marked the beginning of Homixide Gang's journey, featuring raw energy and unique beats.

Homixide Lifestyle (2022)

The breakout album that solidified their place in the trap scene, with hits like "Lifestyle" and "5unna."

Snot or Not (2023)

An experimental album showcasing their versatility and creativity.

5TH AMNDMNT (2023)

A politically charged album with hard-hitting tracks and thought-provoking lyrics.

i5u5we5 (2024)

A futuristic album that pushes the boundaries of their sound.

Homixide Lifestyle 2 (2025)

The highly anticipated sequel to their breakout album, showcasing their growth and evolution as artists.
