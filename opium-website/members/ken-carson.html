Ken Carson

About Artist

Ken Carson is a rising star in the Opium Label, known for his energetic performances and innovative sound. He has quickly gained recognition for his unique style and ability to connect with fans through his music.

Biography

Ken Carson, hailing from Atlanta, Georgia, has made a name for himself in the music industry with his bold and experimental approach. His journey began with underground mixtapes, eventually leading to collaborations with prominent artists and a growing fanbase. Ken's music reflects his dynamic personality and creative vision.
