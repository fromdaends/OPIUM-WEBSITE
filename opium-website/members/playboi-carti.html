Playboi Carti

About Artist

Playboi Carti revolutionized and created a new genre of rage/trap music. He built the very foundation that new gen underground rappers stand on today. His style has been reused and modified thousands of times in different rappers. He is known for his unique vocal style, ad-libs, and fashion sense.

Biography

Born in Atlanta, Georgia, Playboi Carti grew up immersed in the vibrant music scene. He began his career under the name Sir Cartier before adopting the moniker Playboi Carti. His debut mixtape gained significant attention, leading to collaborations with major artists. Over the years, Carti's style evolved, embracing experimental sounds and bold fashion statements. His influence extends beyond music, shaping trends in both the underground and mainstream scenes.
