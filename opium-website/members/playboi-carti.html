Playboi Carti

The creator and founder of the label and rap group OPIUM.

Playboi Carti revolutionized and created a new genre of rage/trap music.

He built the very foundation that new gen underground rappers stand on today. His style has been reused and modified thousands of times in different rappers.

He is known for his unique vocal style, ad-libs, and fashion sense. You could even say if it wasnt for him, underground music wouldnt exist today.
